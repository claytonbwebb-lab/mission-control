About Us

Personal Mechanics At Your Service

Auto Tech Services has been at the heart of Ashton-under-Lyne's motoring community for over 25 years. We're not a faceless chain — we're your local experts who know you, know your car, and take genuine pride in every job we do.

Specialising in VW and Audi vehicles while welcoming all makes and models, we combine specialist knowledge with the kind of honest, personal service you just don't find at main dealers. Industry-standard diagnostic tools, experienced mechanics, and a reputation built on trust.

Our loyalty card scheme rewards regular customers with free puncture repairs and exclusive discounts. And our free MOT text reminder service means you'll never accidentally drive on an expired MOT again.
